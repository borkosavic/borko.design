Belimo Assistant

A cross-platform app for HVAC setup, diagnostics, and management across multiple devices and connection protocols.

Duration 2023 (1 year)
Skills UX Design, UI Design, Design System
URL belimo.com/assistant-app
Project Hero Image

Project Overview

Belimo Assistant is a cross-platform app (web, mobile, and native) designed to facilitate the commissioning, setup, diagnostics, and management of Belimo HVAC products.

The primary users were Belimo employees and subcontractors, including HVAC setup, commissioning, and maintenance professionals. They were expert users, accustomed to technical workflows but often limited by environmental challenges.

My Role

  • • Migrated design system to Figma
  • • Designed user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes
  • • Collaborated closely with developers and QA

Key Challenges

  • • Supporting three OS, three device types, and seven connection methods
  • • Ensuring consistent user flows across platforms
  • • Handling errors effectively in complex technical environments

Solution

Design System Migration

Built a modern Figma-based design system with auto layout and variables, enabling faster design iterations and better collaboration.

Design System Components

Error Handling Automation

Automated error handling processes, reducing user-facing issues by 80% and improving overall user experience in challenging field conditions.

Error Handling Flow

Real-Device Prototyping

Created prototypes for real-device testing to eliminate guesswork and ensure the app worked effectively in actual field conditions.

Device Testing Setup

Key Findings

Environmental Constraints

Users often work in challenging environments where traditional interaction patterns may not apply.

Need for Rapid Iteration

The technical complexity required frequent testing and refinement of user flows.

Interaction Differences

Different devices and connection methods required unique interaction considerations.

Results

2x Faster Design Process

The new Figma-based design system significantly accelerated the design workflow and reduced iteration time.

Streamlined Collaboration

Centralized collaboration in Figma improved communication between design, development, and QA teams.

Seamless User Experience

Despite the technical complexity of supporting multiple platforms and connection methods, we delivered a consistent and intuitive user experience that met the needs of expert users in challenging field conditions.
